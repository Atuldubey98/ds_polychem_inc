About DS Polychem INC

DS Polychem Inc. is a growing chemical manufacturing company committed to delivering reliable, high-performance, and customized chemical solutions for diverse industrial applications. With a strong focus on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we work closely with our clients to support their processes with products they can trust.

Our manufacturing facility is located in Bhiwadi, Rajasthan, a well-established industrial hub with excellent connectivity and infrastructure. This strategic location enables us to ensure efficient production, consistent quality, and timely supply across India.

Our plant is designed to support scalable production, enabling us to meet both current requirements and future growth.

DS Polychem Inc. is conscious of its responsibility toward the environment and society. We continuously strive to adopt responsible manufacturing practices, focusing on efficient resource use, reduced waste, and safer processes.

    Products at a glance

    Fire Resistance Enhancer

    DS Polychem Inc. offers a Fire Resistance Enhancer designed as a 100% functional replacement for Antimony Trioxide (ATO) in flame-retardant formulations. The product is developed to provide effective flame-retardant performance while improving formulation flexibility. This product has been formulated through application-based testing and continuous technical development to ensure compatibility with commonly used polymer and coating systems.

    Calcium Stearate

    DS Polychem Inc. manufactures Calcium Stearate, a calcium salt of stearic acid, widely used as a lubricant, stabilizer, and processing aid across multiple industries. The product is designed to deliver consistent processing performance, thermal stability, and moisture resistance. Calcium Stearate is suitable for applications requiring reliable lubrication and improved process efficiency without negatively impacting end-product properties.

    Emulsifier

    DS Polychem Inc. offers an Emulsifier and Lubrication Enhancer designed to improve internal and external lubrication across a wide range of industrial processes. The product is developed to enhance processing efficiency, mechanical performance, and electrical properties, while supporting higher production line speeds. This emulsifier is suitable for applications where smooth processing, reduced friction, and stable material flow are critical.
